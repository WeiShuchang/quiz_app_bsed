Objectives

General Objective

The primary objective of this application is to facilitate users in learning and practicing operations related to fractions.

Specific Objectives

This application specifically aims to assist users in understanding the fundamental concepts of fractions and to develop proficiency in the following areas:

  • Differentiating between various types of fractions
  • Converting improper fractions to mixed fractions
  • Converting mixed fractions to improper fractions
  • Simplifying fractions to their simplest form
  • Adding both similar and dissimilar fractions
  • Subtracting both similar and dissimilar fractions
  • Multiplying simple fractions
  • Dividing simple fractions

Learning Competencies

These objectives align with some learning competencies outlined in the K-12 Curriculum. The table below presents the learning competencies covered in the application:

Grade Level Learning Competencies
Grade 4 (Second Quarter)
  • Identifies proper fractions, improper fractions, and mixed numbers.
  • Changes improper fraction to mixed numbers and vice versa.
  • Changes fractions to lowest forms.
  • Performs addition and subtraction of similar and dissimilar fractions.
Grade 5 (First Quarter)
  • Multiplies a fraction and a whole number and another fraction.
  • Divides simple fractions.
Grade 7 (First Quarter)
  • Performs operations on rational numbers.
