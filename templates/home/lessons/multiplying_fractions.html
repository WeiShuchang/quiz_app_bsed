Multiplying Fractions

Definition: Let a b and c d are fractions, then their product is given by ac bd . It is straight forward process of multiplying the numerators and then multiplying the denominators.

Note: In terms wherein a whole number is multiplied to a fraction or a mixed number is multiplied to a fraction or another mixed number, convert these numbers first into fraction form then proceed with usual multiplication.

Example 1:

  • 3 4 × 1 2 = 3(1) 4(2) = 3 8

Example 2:

  • (- 5 6 ) × 2 7 = -5(2) 6(7) = -10 42 = -5 21 or - 5 21

Example 3:

  • 3 × 3 4 = 3 1 × 3 4 = 3(3) 1(4) = 9 4 or or can be expressed as 2 1 4

Example 4:

  • 2 3 4 × 2 5 = 11 4 × 2 5 = 11(2) 4(5) = 22 20 or or can be expressed as 1 1 10

Example 5:

  • 6 1 4 × (-2 2 3 ) = 25 4 × -4 3 = 25(-4) 4(3) = -100 12 or -25 4 or can be expressed into -8 1 3
